How To Play!

The goal of Blackjack is to bea the dealer's hand without going over 21. In Blackjack, every player is competing against the dealer- not against each other. In each round, the player's goal is to get a hand that's higher than dealer's(without being higher than 21 or "busting"). Only by beating the dealer can a player win their bet. Players determine the value of their hand by tallying up the point values of their cards.
